Dream Park History and Location

Dreampark, established in 1999, is the leading amusement park in Egypt and the Middle East. Designed by the renowned Canadian firm Forrec, creators of Universal Studios and Mall of America, the park spans 150 acres in Dreamland, 6th of October City. As a member of IAAPA, Dreampark offers entertainment for all ages with 31 rides, including 9 for kids and 10 thrilling rides, plus a simulator theater. The park also features over 15 food outlets, 5 souvenir shops, and a convenient train for easy access throughout the park.

Why Dreampark is your destination for Fun?

The park also includes two theaters ( Children thetre & A Roman theatre) which present wonderful and interesting shows. Being more unique, the park comprises the most largest concert area . Safety on all rides and equipment is assured with safety certification from the world famous T.U.V. Rhineland. Dreampark can accommodate more than 30000 visitors per day, Tickets prices give an unlimited access to all rides and games.
